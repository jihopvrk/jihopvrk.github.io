brainstorm

2019

Brainstorm is an ongoing project where I look into the specifics of the fashion industry. In this day and age it feels like fashion is always being repeated and everything looks the same. Using elements of recognizable brands, I create shoes to show that the industry is somewhat boring.

deja-vu

2019

The beginning concept when I was doing these pieces was that I wanted to create a surreal looking environment while taking inspiration from Japan, Nintendo, and older movies. I used Photoshop to compile images together then used the image trace in illustrator. I used the high fidelity option.

error in code

2019

I wanted to blend the natural form of an animal with robot forms as well as a human element when making this piece. I also used the sound editor to glitch this photo and it came out in a very muted color and has fragments that are not seen in the before photo and is the reason why this piece is called error in code.
